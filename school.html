ABOUT MY SCHOOL

Alwin Memorial Public School (AMPS) was started in the year 2010 in memory of V. Alwin, the beloved son of Dr. N.Vijayan, the founder of Zion group of schools. The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education. It is a co-educational institution with English as the medium of instructions. The aim of the school is to provide educational experience and value education to the students in all aspects of life.
